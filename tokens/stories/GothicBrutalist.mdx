# Gothic Brutalist

Ordo Noctis is a gothic brutalist design system. Dark is canonical. Corners are zero. Strokes are 1.5px.

## Palette families

- **Void** Near-black grounds, from pure `#000` to `#2a2830`. `void.050` (`#07060a`) is the canonical page background.
- **Bone** Aged-paper foregrounds. `bone.600` (`#ebe6e4`) is primary offwhite. Pure white is banned.
- **Blood** Deep gothic reds. `blood.400` (`#9f0d17`) is the primary mark. `blood.600` is signal red.
- **Ember** Smoldering accents for warnings. Used sparingly.

## Type

- **Display** Cormorant Garamond. Tight leading (0.92), negative tracking (-0.01em).
- **Sans** Space Grotesk. Body, UI, all-caps labels.
- **Mono** JetBrains Mono. Code, dates, tabular data.
- **Blackletter** Pirata One. Logotype and ritual accents (e.g. 404).

## Motion

One easing only: `cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.6, 0.2, 1)`. Durations: 160ms / 320ms / 800ms / 1333ms.

## Strokes and radii

`1.5px` regular, `3px` heavy. Zero radius everywhere. Avatars are squares. Buttons are rectangles.
